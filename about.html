Our Story

Bean’s Dreams began as a small spark — a belief that movement and music could help people reconnect with their inner strength. Over the years, that spark grew into a vibrant community of dancers, musicians, and expressive artists of all ages.

What started as a simple dream has become a mission-driven space where students feel seen, supported, and inspired. Whether you’re stepping into dance for the first time, rediscovering your voice, or seeking healing through movement, Bean’s Dreams is a place where you can grow at your own pace.

Every class is an invitation to rise into who you were always meant to be.

Meet the Founder

🦋

Rebecca “Bean” — Founder, Teacher, Guide

Rebecca is a mentor who blends dance, music, and trauma‑informed healing to guide others toward resilience. Her recovery from narcissistic abuse deepened her commitment to emotional support and gave her the courage to pursue a second career as a firefighter — a role embodying strength, service, and protection.

Today, her mission is to empower others through joyful expression, authentic healing, and the reminder that it is possible to rise from adversity into a life of purpose and power.

🎸

Nick — Instructor, Musician, Coach

Nick grew up in the dance business and brings over 40 years of experience teaching dance and gymnastics. He’s also a lifelong musician and coach, having spent his summers as a teen in Los Angeles coaching Olympic gymnasts and performing in bands.

Nick has worked for Hanna-Barbera and Disney and now shares his gifts through personalized instruction in guitar, singing, and movement.

Our Journey

Bean’s Dreams started as a small collection of online classes designed to help students reconnect with joy.
As more students joined, the program expanded into dance, guitar, vocal coaching, and adult movement.
Bean’s Dreams continues to evolve, offering creative, healing-centered programs for all ages.
